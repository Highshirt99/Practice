Vanilla Cake

Vanilla cake, though not really a meal,is something I reallly enjoy eating. It's basically made with flower, and best served with a cold drink.
Recipe
S/N Item Measurement
1 Butter 250g
2 Sugar 1.5 cups
3 Egg 6
4 Flour 2.5 cups
5 Vanilla Flavor 1 teaspoon
6 Baking Powder 1 teaspoon
Methods
  1. Mix the butter and sugar till the color changes, and it becomes fluffy.
  2. Seperate the egg yolk from egg white.
  3. Put the yolk inside the butter and sugar mixture, and mix again.
  4. Put add vanilla flavor to the egg white, and whisk till it becomes foamy.
  5. Sieve flour and add baking powder to it, mix the two.
  6. Pour the liquid mixture and the powder mixture into the butter and sugar, simultaneously, and mix.
  7. Rub a baking tin with butter and flour, then pour the batter inside.
  8. Bake at a moderate temperature, for at least 1hr.
